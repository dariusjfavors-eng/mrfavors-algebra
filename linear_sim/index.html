Linear Graphing Simulator (Student Edition)

Follow the steps to graph the equation y = mx + b.

Current Equation:

Slope/Growth (m) & Starting Value (b)

Steps:

  1. Turn on your calculator.
  2. Press [y=].
  3. Type your equation into the first "y=" line. (Use [÷] or [ALPHA]+[Y=] for fractions!)
  4. Press [Graph] to see the function.
  5. Press [2nd] then [Graph] to get a table. Use arrows to scroll.
  6. Plot at least 2 whole number points on the large graph.
  7. Connect the points with the "Draw Line" button.
  8. Put arrows on the line with the "Add Arrows" button.
