  • The top of the X shows the product — two numbers must multiply to it
  • The bottom of the X shows the sum — those same two numbers must add to it
  • Finding these two numbers lets you factor the quadratic using the AC/X Method

Pick Two Bottles

  • 🧪 Click any two bottle tiles from the bank below the X-board
  • ✅ Selected tiles glow gold — click a selected tile again to deselect it
  • ⚗️ Once two tiles are selected, press "Check Answer" — the cauldron reacts instantly
  • 💡 Watch for negative numbers — a negative product means one factor is negative

Hints & Feedback

  • 🔥 Correct answer: The cauldron sparkles and your streak grows
  • 💨 Wrong sign only: You get partial credit — the numbers are right, signs are flipped
  • 💡 Hint button: Use up to 3 hints per puzzle — each one gets more specific
  • 🏆 Earn coins for correct answers and spend them in the Shop for new themes
