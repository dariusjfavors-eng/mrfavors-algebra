Your Mission, Should You Choose to Accept It...

You are an expert safe-cracker. Your target: the legendary Algebra Vault.

To find the 4-part combination, you must first sort the jumbled polynomial terms (the "loot") from the top bar into their correct bins. The total from each bin is one number of the combination. As you sort, you'll see the totals compute themselves!

Get the combination right, and the "Keys to the Regents" are yours. Good luck, thief.
